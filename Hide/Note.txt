
Game Mode:
- Player controls the Blue car (HidingCar) with arrow keys.
- Goal is to evade the AI AttackerCar (Red).
- AI AttackerCar uses A* pathfinding to chase the Player.
- A laser visualizes the Attacker's Line of Sight (LOS).
- Game ends when the Attacker has a clear LOS to the Player within laser range.

Potential Improvements:
- Make attacker AI more sophisticated (e.g., use prediction, smarter pathing).
- Add scoring based on survival time.
- Improve game over screen/restart functionality.
- Add sound effects.
